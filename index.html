Hackathon Journey

My Experience at the SIH Hackathon

During my participation in the Smart India Hackathon (SIH) 2024, I had the opportunity to work with an incredible team to create a digital platform aimed at connecting vendors and buyers of agricultural by-products. These by-products include livestock, manure, and reusable waste, and the platform seeks to enhance sustainability by reducing intermediaries and ensuring fair pricing. As the Frontend Developer of the team, I was responsible for creating intuitive and user-friendly interfaces, which allowed for seamless interactions on the platform. The experience was challenging but rewarding as we faced tight deadlines and technical obstacles. However, through teamwork and effective leadership, we were able to stay on track and present a working prototype that incorporated video call support and QR code-based delivery verification. This project has not only helped me improve my technical skills but also provided me with valuable experience in team coordination and presenting innovative solutions to real-world problems.
